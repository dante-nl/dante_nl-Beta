<h2><a>

Custime — make the time yours

</h2></a>

Custime is a simple clock. Literally, that's it. But, it has a trick up its sleeve! Customisation. "Custime" is a mix between the words "custom" and "time". It is extremely easy to use, you can change the font by clicking on the time, switch between world time and date by clicking on the date and you can use the options at the bottom to customise it even further! Custimes are also very easy to share! For example, you can use this custime inspired by this website!

You can also use the advanced editor if you would like to customise your custime even further!

<h2><a>

Cuschat — chat with the safety of temporary chats

</h2></a>

Cuschat is a chatting website that allows you to chat with others in the main chat and in rooms. The main chat gets permanently deleted every night at 23:00:00 UTC (click to convert) and the rooms after about one hour of inactivity.

Cuschat also has support for bots. You can see an example of the built-in one by typing -help in any chat or by going to archyve.dantenl.tk as this is a great example of an external service accessing Cuschat's messages.
